Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Bible Study Connect.

When will Bible Study Connect launch?

We're currently in development and plan to launch a beta version in the coming days. Join our waitlist to be notified when we're ready!

Is Bible Study Connect free to use?

Yes, the core features of Bible Study Connect will always be free!, our goal is to build a large community of Bible study groups and make Bible study accessible to everyone.

Which denominations does Bible Study Connect support?

We welcome all Christian denominations on our platform. Users can specify their denomination in their profile and filter studies accordingly.

Can I host both virtual and in-person Bible studies?

Absolutely! Bible Study Connect supports both virtual and in-person formats, with tools tailored to each type.

How will I be notified about updates?

When you join our waitlist, you'll receive occasional email updates about our development progress and launch timeline.
